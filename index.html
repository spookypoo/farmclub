2026 Season
"Yesterday's Traditions, Today's Health, Tomorrow's Hope"
The Green Goose Farming Club offers a hands-on opportunity to learn, grow, and contribute to a working regenerative farm. Members gain real experience creating their own intensive vegetable garden — from soil preparation and succession planting through to the fall harvest.
What the Club Offers
🌱
Access to local historic farmland with deep agricultural roots
🚜
Irrigation and tools provided — everything you need to get growing
🤝
Skill & community building alongside fellow growers and stewards
🥬
Fresh, high-quality vegetables shared between club members

Members work independently and collaboratively to cultivate a variety of vegetables throughout the season.
All work is done by hand, peasant fashion. The harvest is shared between club members and Green Goose Farm,
reflecting the shared effort and care invested in the land.
🌾 Who Can Join
Open to anyone with an interest in sustainable agriculture — farmers, gardeners, students, and nature lovers alike. No prior experience required, just enthusiasm and dedication. Applicants should be fit, highly organized, and open to learning.
🐾 Our Animals & Land
Green Goose is a diverse animal farm guided by sound ecological practices. Members become stewards of a thriving, resilient ecosystem — blending time-honored traditions with regenerative methods that restore the land for future generations.
